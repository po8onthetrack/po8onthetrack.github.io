Pose-Based Human Action Recognition

APS360 Deep Learning Project · In progress, 2026

A deep-learning system that recognizes athletic movement from body pose alone: given a short sequence of 17 body keypoints tracked from a single camera, it classifies the action — no motion-capture gear. Inspired by badminton and bouldering, aimed at movement analysis and injury-aware coaching.

  • Trains an RNN over 30-frame pose sequences (MPOSE2021, 20 action classes) in PyTorch.
  • Normalizes & cleans keypoints — hip-centering, torso scaling, confidence masking — with a subject-independent, leakage-aware split.
  • Benchmarks against an MLP baseline on time-averaged pose; evaluated with accuracy & macro-F1.
